Photography Intern – collaboration / Unpaid

Photography Intern – collaboration / Unpaid

Collaboration, Internship - Unpaid

Winnipeg Posted 3 weeks ago

Want to be a part of something that will change the way people Shop? Are you highly organized, a strong communicator, a people’s person and an upcoming photographer? Above all, are you willing to sacrifice the short-term for the long-term?

If yes, then we would like to hear from you. Canadian E Market (SkipTheMall), a local On-Demand Online shopping and Delivery Service startup is looking to hire a Photography Intern in an unpaid collaboration role (either for experience, School Credit or fun), to help take quality product photos, event photos, and social media/Ads/banner photos.

Canadian E Market (SkipTheMall)

Canadian E Market has created a way for shoppers to Skip the Mall, traffic, long drives or checkout queues on busy days, and do all their shopping from either a single local store or multiple local stores in one order, from the comfort of their homes or offices or from anywhere in the city and have their orders delivered in minutes or at a time of their choosing. You can call us “The Uber for shopping”. We run errands, so shoppers can be more productive with their precious time. Through the Canadian E Market App and platform, local stores now have the opportunity to reach customers at all ends of the city, making it possible for shoppers and local stores within the same city, to access each other more easily by eliminating the factors that affect shopping, like distance, weather, busy schedules, shipping wait time (having to wait up to 2 days for your orders to be delivered, when you shop online with major online retail giants) etc.

Company Objective

Our Objective is to simplify shopping for Canadians and to help local brick and mortar retail stores increase their city-wide reach and provide them with tools and a very seamless way to fully participate in the constantly growing online shopping industry in Canada, while being able to maintain their physical, local locations.

Creative and possess excellent communication skills

Strong eye for design and visual content

Very interested in trends

Passionate

Team player

Confident about connecting with people and can confidently express an idea

Confident in answering questions and inquiries.

* Having a prior background in photography, content creation or social media is an asset, but not required.

Disclaimer

This is a 4-6 months unpaid internship/collaborations, and has the potential for renewal and/or full-time/part-time paid employment status in a management position, at the end of this period. You must be willing to attend the Company’s strategy meeting, at least once a week and work remotely with you own flexible schedules.
Requirement
This position requires you to have your own laptop.

How to Apply

Please send your resume and cover letter to admin@canadianemarket.ca , using the subject “PHOTOGRAPHY INTERN – COLLABORATION / UNPAID”. OR Apply Online by submitting your resume on the company’s website: https://canadianemarket.ca/ca/jobs
