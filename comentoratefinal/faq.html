Frequently Asked Questions

What is CoMentorate?

CoMentorate is a live streaming mentor community focused on immediately helping you learn & grow.

How does it work?

Join a live mentor to ask your questions in real time alongside other members by typing out your message. If you like their content, subscribe & browse their shop without ever leaving the stream.

Can anyone be a mentor?

Yes, we think growth & wisdom are gained by living life, & every person has valuable knowledge to share with others.

The site is free. Subscription options & shop purchases are available.
