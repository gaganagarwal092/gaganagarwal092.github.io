About Dynamik Carpet Cleaning

Warren Clark founded Dynamik Carpet Cleaning in 1992 with one simple goal in mind: to provide professional, cost-effective, high-level carpet cleaning services to clients across America.

For almost 30 years our company has cleaned thousands of carpets for all types of clients in their homes and businesses. We have countless happy customers.

We are five stars rated and plan to stay five stars rated for the next 30 years and beyond by providing top-notch service.
