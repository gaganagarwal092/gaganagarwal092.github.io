Frequently Asked Questions about our services.

General Questions

How much does it cost to clean XYZ?

Our pricing is based on the square footage of the area that you need to get cleaned, plus the depth of the work involved.

For example, a relatively clean carpet that simply needs a once over clean is going to cost much less than a dirty and soiled carpet that has been left for years in a vacant property.

Call us today and we will happily quote you a price over the phone.

What is your avalibility?

We run a 24-hour emergency service. If you have a cleaning emergency call us and we will get you booked in for our next available appointment.

What areas do you service?

Dynamik Carpet Cleaning is a national company. We service multiple cities across the US and soon will be expanding to Canada.

Carpet Cleaning

What cleaning method do you use?

It depends on the situation but generally, we use deep steam cleaning. Also known as hot water extraction.

This is an advanced cleaning process that injects 200-degree steam into the carpet while simultaneously sucking out 95% of the moisture instantly. While also removing all germs, grime, and bacteria at the same time.

While this is by far the most effective cleaning method available today. Different circumstances call for different cleaning methods. We do use other methods depending on the situation.

How long will my carpets take to dry after cleaning?

The short answer is 4 to 6 hours.

The long answer is it really depends. If you can’t ventilate an area properly or you require carpet cleaning in the middle of winter the carpets can take up to twice as long to dry.

How much does carpet cleaning cost?

Each job is different. Does your house have two bedrooms or four? Is there a basement that needs cleaning or perhaps a living room?

The same applies to business locations. Businesses can be a small five-person office to a 5,000 square foot showroom floor.

Please call us and we will happily provide you a quote over the phone.

Upholstery Cleaning

What types of upholstery do you clean?

Everything! No matter what needs cleaning we can clean it as long as it is cleanable. There are certain types of materials that are not cleanable but for the most part, all general store bought furniture can be cleaned.

What method do you use to clean furniture?

Each fabric has a different cleaning method. It’s not possible to describe them all here. Please call us to discuss your needs.

How often should upholstery be cleaned?

Generally speaking, upholstery that is used on a regular basis should be cleaned every 12 months.

Rug Cleaning

What types of rugs can you clean?

All of them! Oriental rugs, Seagrass rugs, Moroccan, Tibetan, Dhurrie rugs, Persian rugs, Sisal, Flokati, Navajo, and even cut carpet and boat rugs.

How long does rug cleaning take?

Make sure your rug looks perfect is a much more delicate process that carpet cleaning. Rugs take about one week from pick up (or drop off) to completion.

Do I have to drop off the rug or do you pick up?

It’s totally up to you. We can pick-up the rug or you can drop-off the rug at our facility. Whatever is easiest for you is easiest for us!
