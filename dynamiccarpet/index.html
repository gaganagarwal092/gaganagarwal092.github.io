What type of carpet, rugs, and upholstery do you handle?

At Dynamik Carpet Cleaning, we clean anything and everything! We cover all common household carpets and even specialty carpet. When it comes to rug cleaning we cover Persian, Oriental, Moroccan, Tibetan, Indian, Runners, Area Rugs, Circular Rugs, Sculptured and many more. This is just a small list. If you don’t see your rug just call and ask!

We also clean any and all kinds of upholstery.

What carpet cleaning method do you use?

For the most part, we use Deep Steam Carpet Cleaning which is also known as Hot Water Extraction. This cleaning method is an advanced process that “injects” pressurized hot water into the carpet at about 200 degrees. This allows even the deepest of dirt and grime to be extracted.

At the same time, powerful suction is working to remove up to 95% of the moisture that was injected into the carpet while also removing all the unsanitary germs and bacteria that have built up over time in carpets.

We do use other cleaning methods depending on the situation. This will all be discussed you when you call us for an obligation free quote.

How much does carpet cleaning cost?

As every home and business is different, it makes it each job different. This means that the cost of carpet cleaning and related services will also be different from job to job. Please call us and we will happily quote you over the phone.
