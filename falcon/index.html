THE BRIGHT TALENT

Falcone, a Luminokinetic artist Falcone is a young artist, self-taught, 28 years old, from Paris. He exhibited his works in several famous galleries, from Paris to San Francisco.

This artist recently exhibited his luminokinetic works at the mythical Hôtel Raphaël in Paris and at the prestigious Plaza Athénée on Avenue Montaigne.

Inspired by the love that his collector grandfather always brought to the luminous and kinetic art, the artist takes again in his way the neon and the plexiglass, the mirror and the LED.

The proposed works are mostly geometric shapes and kinetic inspiration. However, "on- demand" works have been created. A guitar or other object that is close to the customer's heart can be inserted.

The works are murals or directly positioned on the ground. The LED allows the color change of the piece as well as its intensity by using a remote control.
