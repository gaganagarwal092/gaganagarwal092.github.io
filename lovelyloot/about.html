Hi there!

I’m Ida, the founder of Lovelyloot. Beauty has also seemed like an amazingly powerful tool to me – not to look a certain way, but because of how it made me feel. Lovelyloot truly began as the solution to my personal problem, and that’s why it’s designed to fit your real life.

Lovelyloot is a champion for positivity & inclusivity in beauty. We believe in the power of beauty and the confidence it inspires within each of us.

We see beauty as a lifelong journey, where there isn’t just one path. We say beauty isn’t a mask to hide behind – it’s a tool to present yourself to the world however you choose, to create the identity you desire, to embrace what you were both with.

We know that taking pride in how you look & feel isn’t superficial, vain or self-absorbed.

It’s saying that you value yourself as much as you value others…

and that’s a pretty powerful way to live.

Lovelyloot will truly make your life easier!
