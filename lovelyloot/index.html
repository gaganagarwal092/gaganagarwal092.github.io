BEAUTY IS PERSONAL

So, you have a thing for beauty...we get that.

Just like you organize your stash at home, you need a way to virtually keep track of all those items plus the products you love or want to try. Otherwise, you end up with a zillion wish lists, notes and screenshots all over the place...making it hard to find that information later when you need it.

Save any product, from anywhere

You find it and we'll help you save it. Search thousands of beauty brands, from luxury to drugstore to indie. Even if we don't have it, it's been discontinued or hasn't launched yet, no problem – simply upload your own image or product to capture anything.

Upgrade your beauty organization

We go way beyond your typical Wishlist. You can also create unlimited personal collections – like your current skincare routine, a list of the foundations you want to try or even your product fails. Make your collections beautifully sharable by adding images, a description and YouTube / blog / Instagram links (and potentially get featured on Lovelyloot!).

Personal notes are everything!

Finding the right products that work for you can definitely be a journey. Add personal notes to any product so you remember your shade, exactly what you liked (or didn’t!) or why you wanted to try it in the first place.

It’s all about shopping smarter.

Whether you’re a student, mom or CEO (or all three!), no one likes wasting time or money. Lovelyloot will help you shop smarter, so you buy the things you really want, not just more stuff. Find all your information and inspiration right in your Loot and feel great about your next purchase.

Lovelyloot will truly make your life easier!
