How does it work

Make decisions based on data.

Metric.ai estimates hours spent on projects by integrating with your existing services and looking into work that was done. Whether it's code commit in GitHub, updated ticket in JIRA or conversation in Slack â€“ Metric.ai algorithms will use this information to predict efforts spent on a project.

Measure what matters for success.

By linking estimated efforts to financial data, Metric.ai calculates projects expenses, revenue, and profitability. Use historical data to see the past and present. See the future by looking at forecast which is calculated based on project state and planned resources.

Learn More

Maintain a good balance.

By splitting work into internal and billable, Metric.ai calculates your utilization. Whether it's for the entire organization, department, role or particular team member.
