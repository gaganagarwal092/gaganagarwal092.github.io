About

Alex tried to find a way to reduce the risk of injury in the fitness domain after he tore his cruciate ligament. Fitness is a sport that needs to be learned like any else - however, most people can’t afford a professional fitness coach. With Joel and Caspar, Alex found two passionate co-founders that shared his vision to develop sports technologies that improve people’s life’s. In collaboration with the University of Zürich they created a technology for safe and highly effective training - VAY was born.
