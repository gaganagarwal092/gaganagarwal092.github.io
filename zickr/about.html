Who we are

We believe in giving individual attention to each of our clients, and building a solid & lasting relationship based on trust and satisfaction. We do not believe in using templates or creating cookie-cutter designs. You deserve branding as which is inique to your business. We dont believe in completing a project and disappearing after. We believe in supporting and maintaining our work. After all, your brands design is a reflection of us, and your satisfaction is very importan. We aim to provide a spectrum of services and do so with professionalism, enthusiasm & dedication. One of our primary goals is to make the design process itself enjoyable for our clients.

Zickr started in 2017 as a small full-stack Marketing and development agency with its focus in design. We came together to form a team, of professionals from multidisciplinary backgrounds working towards one goal to provide unique, quality services to our clients and maketheir brand stand out in the crowd.
